# Tier 5 Graduation Benchmark

Tier 5 is Sergeant's trust benchmark.

It does not claim Sergeant is better because of features. It measures whether Sergeant has earned trust on real review work.

## Scorecard

- Real bugs found: 25
- False-positive control: 20
- Explanation quality: 15
- Architecture reasoning: 15
- Security findings: 10
- Regression prediction: 10
- Documentation consistency: 5

## Verdicts

- `GRADUATED`
- `TRUSTED_WITH_WATCH`
- `NEEDS_MORE_PROOF`

## Rule

Sergeant graduates by earned evidence across real reviews, not by feature claims.

External reviewers can be used as benchmark references, but they are only measuring sticks. Sergeant must work and reach its own verdict without them.
